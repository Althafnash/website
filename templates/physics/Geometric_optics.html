3.Transmission of Light though objects

We can do an activity to find out how Light transmits thought different objects

We will need

  1.  A metal sheets
  2.  An oil papers
  3.  A piece of decorative glass
  4.  A small piece of wooden sheet
  5.  A thin piece of glass
  6.  different types of polyethene sheet
  7.  a black colour papers
  8.  Tissues of different colours
  9.  A piece of newspaper

Method

  1.  Cut a square of 3cm by 3cm in the middle of the carboard
  2.  Light the candle
  3.  Keep each of the objects middle of the cardboard and the flame
  4.  Move the objects toward the flame and observe the flame from the object

Observation

  1.  In some object the flame and Light can be seen
  2.  In some objects the Light can be see but the flame cannot be seen
  3.  In some object both Light and flame cannot be seen

Conclusion

  1.  The objects where the observed object and Light can be seen is called transparent object
  2.  The objects where the observed object cannot be seen but Light can be seen is called translucentobject
  3.  The objects where the observed object and Light cannot be seen is called opaque object
