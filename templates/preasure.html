Preasure

Preasure is the force excerted over a unit area

Preasure is affected by two things namely

  • Force
  • Surface area

The preasure execerted by a solid object on a solid surface depends on the surface area on which it is acting

Higher the surface area on which the force is acting the lower the preasure

Lower the surface area on which the force is acting higher the preasur

  • preasure = perpendicular force / surface area
